Manage Accounts

The Smart Power Monitoring and Control System was developed to help track and manage the flow of current in the devices installed at various stations controlled by Total Energies. The Manager of any station registered on this system can remotely mitigate risks of power surges through the devices by cutting of the flow, and subsequently reduce downtime and losses. The system automatically generates periodic reports on the health status of all linked devices to help managers direct work on their respective stations easily as well as to maintain records.
